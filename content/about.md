### Introduction
Dascoda evolved from ownership, operational accountability, and enterprise infrastructure leadership. As technology ecosystems became more complex, the need for practical guidance and execution discipline became increasingly clear.

### Timeline
- 2006: Hospitality Ownership & Operations
  - Technology oversight, infrastructure planning, vendor management, and operational accountability within hospitality ownership environments.
- 2017-2020: Provider Complexity Accelerates
  - Provider consolidation, increasing service portfolios, mobility growth, and more complex stakeholder environments create new operational challenges.
- 2020-2024: Connectivity & Mobility Convergence
  - Connectivity, mobility, Wi-Fi, infrastructure, and technology initiatives become increasingly interconnected across multi-site organizations.
- Today: Dascoda Solutions
  - Founder-led guidance helping ownership groups, operators, and technology leaders evaluate options, align stakeholders, coordinate providers, and maintain accountability from planning through execution.

## A Market in Transition
### Headline
More scale did not always mean more continuity.

### Copy
As technology ecosystems expanded, many organizations gained access to more providers, products, and purchasing options. At the same time, accountability, continuity, and operational alignment often became harder to maintain.

### Comparison
Then:
- Dedicated account relationships
- Vertical specialization
- Fewer technology decisions
- Direct accountability
- Simpler migrations

Now:
- Multiple providers and stakeholders
- Broader service portfolios
- Increasing infrastructure complexity
- Shared ownership across teams
- Multi-vendor coordination

### Why It Matters
Organizations today need more than access to technology solutions. They need experienced guidance that aligns stakeholders, coordinates providers, and maintains accountability from evaluation through execution.
