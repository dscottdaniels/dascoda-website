## Solution Components
### Passpoint-enabled Ameriband Wi-DAS
Ameriband's productized Passpoint-enabled solution using existing enterprise Wi-Fi infrastructure to support secure authentication, Wi-Fi Calling, carrier integration, reporting, and improved indoor wireless experiences.

### Passpoint
The Wi-Fi Alliance standard behind secure, automatic connectivity for supported devices on participating Wi-Fi networks without repeated logins or captive portals.

### Other Indoor Options
When needed, Dascoda helps clients consider DAS, small cells, private wireless, neutral host, or other approaches based on property requirements.
