Discuss Ameriband Wi-DAS

What is Passpoint?

Secure, automatic Wi-Fi connection for supported devices.

Passpoint is a Wi-Fi Alliance standard that allows supported devices to connect securely and automatically to participating Wi-Fi networks without repeated logins or captive portals.

What is Ameriband Wi-DAS?

A productized Passpoint-enabled approach for hospitality environments.

Ameriband Wi-DAS uses Passpoint and existing enterprise Wi-Fi infrastructure to help support secure authentication, Wi-Fi Calling, carrier-integrated connectivity, reporting, and improved indoor wireless experiences.
