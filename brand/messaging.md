# Messaging Notes

## Core Positioning
Dascoda Solutions provides Connectivity, Mobility, and Technology Solutions for Hospitality and Multi-Site Environments.

Dascoda helps organizations evaluate, source, deploy, and support connectivity, mobility, and technology solutions through a curated ecosystem of trusted partners and a commitment to accountability from strategy through execution.

Dascoda is hospitality-first, not hospitality-only. Hospitality remains the primary industry and the strongest source of market context, but Dascoda also supports Multifamily / MDU, Commercial Real Estate, Retail, Manufacturing, and other multi-site environments where connectivity, mobility, and operational continuity are critical.

## Lead Solution Areas
Dascoda has two lead solution areas:
- Business Internet & Network Services
- Mobility & Indoor Connectivity

Technology Advisory and Program Leadership are supporting capabilities. They help clients assess options, align stakeholders, coordinate partners, and execute, but they should not be presented as the primary solution categories.

## Audience
- Hospitality
- Multifamily / MDU
- Commercial Real Estate
- Retail
- Manufacturing
- Other multi-site environments

## Tone
- Professional
- Executive
- Hospitality-first
- Trusted advisor
- Partner-oriented
- Clear and practical
- Not overly technical
- Relationship-led
- Grounded in execution

## Voice Guidelines
- Lead with business value before technical detail
- Use hospitality context wherever possible
- Present Dascoda as an independent solutions guide, account support resource, and execution partner
- Emphasize evaluation, sourcing support, deployment coordination, agreement support, and ongoing accountability
- Describe partners as curated, accountable, and aligned to client outcomes
- Keep Passpoint copy accessible to hotel owners, operators, and executives
- Use practical business language: agreements, service continuity, property readiness, rollout coordination, stakeholder alignment, and operational impact

## Avoid
- Dense telecom jargon
- Claims that sound like a product vendor
- Language that makes Dascoda sound like a reseller, agent, or division of Ameriband
- Overpromising technical outcomes
- Casual startup language
- Generic IT consulting language
- Unsupported phrases such as "modern guest experiences"

## Key Message Pillars
### Hospitality-First
Hospitality remains the lead story and primary vertical, with messaging shaped around guest experience, staff operations, ownership priorities, brand standards, and property-level realities.

### Multi-Site Relevance
Dascoda's model also applies to Multifamily / MDU, Commercial Real Estate, Retail, Manufacturing, and other property-centric or multi-site operating environments.

### Trusted Advisory
Independent guidance that begins with business objectives and operational requirements before recommending solutions.

### Dedicated Account Agreement Support
Support for hospitality connectivity service agreements, including renewal planning, partner coordination, service scope clarity, and ongoing account alignment.

### Curated Ecosystem
Access to a curated ecosystem of carriers, technology providers, and delivery partners through a disciplined, relationship-led model.

### Strategy Through Execution
Support from evaluation and sourcing through agreement support, implementation coordination, deployment oversight, rollout leadership, and ongoing accountability.

### Ownership Perspective
Experience across hospitality ownership, enterprise technology leadership, and large-scale program execution informs how Dascoda supports decision-making and delivery.

### Ameriband / Passpoint-Enabled Wi-DAS Partnership
Dascoda is Ameriband's exclusive hospitality partner for Passpoint-enabled Wi-DAS opportunities. Dascoda remains an independent client-centered solutions partner that helps clients evaluate fit, align stakeholders, and coordinate execution around Ameriband-enabled opportunities.

Passpoint should remain visible as an important technology topic, but the broader solution category is Mobility & Indoor Connectivity.
